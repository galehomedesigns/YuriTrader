# CRA Standards for Expenses

When a task involves spending money, categorize it immediately for the `Accountant/Expenses_2026.xlsx` tracker.

- **Meals & Entertainment**: Meetings with clients or partners.
- **Travel**: Trips for pilots or investor pitches.
- **Office Supplies**: Software and AI tools.
- **Professional Fees**: Legal or ID work (e.g., Geni's services).
