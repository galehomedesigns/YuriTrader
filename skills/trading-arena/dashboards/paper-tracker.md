# paper-tracker dashboard

**Shows:** the independent forward paper-test of the Opening Power strategy on
stocks — running scorecard (long signals, triggered trades, win rate, net paper
P&L, sum % return), the triggered paper trades, and recent signal decisions.

**Who reads it:** the operator, to judge whether Opening Power is actually
profitable before risking real money.

**Data pulled from:** `skills/trading-arena/logs/paper_track.jsonl` (written by
`opening_agent/paper_tracker.py`). Read-only. Independent of the live CDP/order
system — no orders, no account, no live state.

**URL:** `https://gx10-087b.tail3abae6.ts.net:8443/paper-tracker.html`

**Regenerated by:** `paper-tracker_cron.sh` → `paper-tracker_update.py` (pure
`render(template, data)` per docs/DASHBOARDS.md).
